## flipswitch
`flipswitch` is an alternative look to the checkbox or the two-option select menu. It can be toggled by a click or a swipe. 

### Properties

Refer to JQueryMobile's [flipswitch](http://api.jquerymobile.com/flipswitch/) Widget.
